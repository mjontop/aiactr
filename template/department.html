Computer Science and Engineering Department

VISION

Imparting Quality Technological Education in the field of Electronics and Communication with Emphasis on Industry Oriented Needs, Research, Ethics and Human Values. 

MISSION

  1. To strive for imparting Quality Technical Education in the field of Computer Science and Engineering with latest amenities.
  2. To strive for providing solutions to industry oriented needs in the field of Computer Science and Engineering.
  3. To strive for contributing Quality Research in relevant areas of Computer Science and Engineering such as Information Security, Data Mining and Data Warehousing, Wireless Networks,Language Processing , Software Testing and Semantic Web.
  4. To implant awareness for promotion of ethics and human values among students.
